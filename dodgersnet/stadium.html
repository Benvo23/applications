Getting There

Address: 1000 Vin Scully Avenue, Los Angeles, CA 90012

By Car:
• From the 5 Freeway: Exit at Stadium Way
• From the 110 Freeway: Exit at Dodger Stadium/Academy Road

Parking:
• General Parking: $30-$50
• Preferred Parking: $50-$75
• Gates open 2 hours before first pitch

Public Transit:
• Dodger Stadium Express: Free shuttle from Union Station and South Bay
• LA Metro: Take Gold Line to Chinatown station, walk or shuttle to stadium

Seating Guide

Seating Levels:
• Field Level: Sections 1-59 (Closest to the action)
• Loge Level: Sections 100-163 (Great sightlines)
• Club Level: Sections 200-257 (Premium amenities)
• Reserve Level: Sections 300-363 (Good value)
• Top Deck: Sections 400-463 (Budget-friendly with great views)

Pro Tip: Sections along the first base side (odd numbers) get shade earlier during day games!

Notable Events Hosted

1962-2025
10 World Series Hosted: 1963, 1965, 1966, 1974, 1977, 1978, 1981, 1988, 2024, 2025
2025
18-Inning World Series Game 3 - Freddie Freeman hits walk-off home run against the Blue Jays in the longest World Series game since 2018. First 18-inning game at Dodger Stadium in 7 years.
2023
Elton John's Farewell Concert performed to sold-out crowd.
2022
MLB All-Star Game returned to Dodger Stadium for the first time since 1980.
2018
18-Inning World Series Game 3 - Max Muncy hits walk-off home run against the Red Sox at 3:30 AM. Longest game in World Series history by time (7 hours, 20 minutes).
1984
Summer Olympics baseball competition venue.
1980
Pope John Paul II celebrated Mass for 50,000 people.
