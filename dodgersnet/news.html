📰 Dodgers News

The latest news and updates from the Los Angeles Dodgers - December 2025.

🏆🏆 BACK-TO-BACK WORLD SERIES CHAMPIONS!
The Dodgers are seeking a historic three-peat in 2026 after winning consecutive titles in 2024 and 2025.
💰
December 19, 2025
Dodgers Hit Record $169M Luxury Tax
The Dodgers' 2025 payroll reached $417.3 million, resulting in a record $169.4 million luxury tax bill. Their two-year tax total of $272.4 million surpasses the Yankees for the first time since the penalty started in 2003.
🎺
December 13, 2025
Edwin Díaz Signs 3-Year, $69M Deal
The Dodgers announced the signing of elite closer Edwin Díaz. "I'm looking to win," Díaz said, explaining why he chose LA. Expect the trumpets to sound at Dodger Stadium in 2026.
💵
December 18, 2025
Postseason Shares Worth $484,748 Each
For the second straight year, Dodgers players receive championship bonuses. The 2025 postseason pool reached nearly $128.2 million, with full shares worth $484,747.57.
📈
December 15, 2025
Dodgers' Deferred Obligations Pass $1 Billion
Win now, pay later: The Dodgers' commitment to deferred payments has exceeded $1 billion, a creative financial strategy that has helped build back-to-back championship rosters.
🔄
December 2025
Tyler Glasnow Trade Rumors Emerge
According to reports, Glasnow "has come up in conversations" between the Dodgers and other teams. While a trade is considered "a long shot," the Dodgers reportedly "would not be opposed to moving" the right-hander.
✈️
December 2025
Michael Siani Claimed Off Waivers
The Dodgers claimed outfielder Michael Siani off waivers from the Braves. Atlanta designated him for assignment when they signed Mike Yastrzemski earlier this week.
⚾
December 2025
Best Rotation in Baseball for 2026
The Dodgers enter 2026 with an elite rotation: Shohei Ohtani, Yoshinobu Yamamoto, Blake Snell, Tyler Glasnow, and Roki Sasaki. Arguably the best starting staff in MLB history.
📝
December 2025
Chuckie Robinson Returns on Minor League Deal
The Dodgers signed catcher Chuckie Robinson to a minor league deal, reuniting with the backstop who spent the bulk of the 2025 season in the organization.
