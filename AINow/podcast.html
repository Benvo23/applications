Ep. 147: "Is Open Source AI Winning?"

In this episode, we explore the rapid rise of open-weight models and what it means for the industry. Our guest — a senior AI researcher at a major tech company — argues that open source has already won the developer mindset, and that closed labs need to find new moats beyond model weights.

We cover: the economics of open vs. closed models, why inference cost matters more than training cost, the regulatory implications of freely available frontier models, and whether "open source AI" is even the right term.
