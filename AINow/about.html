About AI Now

Independent AI journalism for those who build, deploy, and regulate artificial intelligence

We cut through the noise to bring you the stories that matter — no hype, no fear, just clarity. AI Now is read by over 150,000 researchers, engineers, executives, and policymakers every day.

Accuracy First

Every story is fact-checked and technically reviewed. We don't publish rumors, and we correct mistakes publicly and promptly.

Independent

We are not owned by, affiliated with, or funded by any AI company. Our editorial decisions are made independently, always.

Depth Over Speed

We'd rather be right than first. Our deep dives and analysis pieces go beyond the headline to explain what really matters and why.

// Our Story

AI Now was founded in 2024 by a small group of journalists and engineers who believed the AI conversation needed better information. At the time, AI coverage was split between breathless hype and unfounded panic — with very little in between.

We set out to build the publication we wanted to read ourselves: technically informed, editorially independent, and accessible to anyone — whether you're training models or trying to understand what they mean for your industry.

Today, AI Now reaches over 150,000 daily readers across 80 countries. Our team includes former researchers from leading AI labs, investigative journalists from major newsrooms, and policy analysts who've worked in government.

"The AI story is too important to be told only by the companies building it. That's why we exist." — The AI Now Editorial Team
