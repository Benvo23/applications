Airbus A350-1000

The New Standard in Long-Haul Travel.

The Airbus A350-1000 is the cornerstone of our international fleet. Its advanced design offers unparalleled levels of operational efficiency and a 25% reduction in fuel burn and CO₂ emissions. For our passengers, this means a quieter, more spacious cabin with higher ceilings, larger windows, and optimized cabin pressure and humidity for a more refreshing journey.
