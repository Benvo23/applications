Our Journey

From a bold idea to a bridge across the Pacific, discover the milestones that have shaped Con Chim Airlines.

2017

Origins

In 2017, 𝒞𝑜𝓃 𝒞𝒽𝒾𝓂 𝒜𝒾𝓇𝓁𝒾𝓃𝑒𝓈 was founded in the vibrant heart of Ho Chi Minh City. The vision was simple yet ambitious: to create a modern, world-class airline connecting Vietnam directly to the world. Our journey began with an order for our very first aircraft, a state-of-the-art Airbus A350-1000, setting the stage for a new era in long-haul travel.

2020

A Challenging Year

The year 2020 marked the most challenging part of our history. Faced with unprecedented global events, we were pushed to the brink, nearly having to return our only A350-1000 order and losing half of our dedicated employees. It was a period of immense difficulty that tested our resolve and commitment.

late 2021

Our Rise

Persevering through adversity, we finally welcomed our first A350 and proudly launched our inaugural route from Ho Chi Minh City to Los Angeles. This triumph was quickly followed by the delivery of three more A350-1000s, enabling us to launch our second and third routes to San Francisco and Seattle.

2023

The Great Step

With growing momentum, we took a great step forward by adding four more A350-1000s to our fleet. This expansion allowed us to connect Vietnam to the heartland of America, launching new non-stop flights to Dallas, Houston, Chicago, and New York.

2024-2025

The Step to Today

Receiving our final four A350-1000s, we solidified our international presence. We established a new hub in Hanoi, launched Ho Chi Minh City flights to Boston, Paris, and London, and introduced new routes from our Hanoi base to London, Paris, and Washington D.C., truly becoming Vietnam's bridge to the world.

The Future

Leading the Way

Our journey is far from over. Our vision is to become the undisputed leader in the Vietnamese-American market, making Con Chim Airlines the definitive #1 choice for every passenger, giving high hopes of beating Vietnam Airlines. Through relentless focus on customer experience and strategic growth, we aim to dominate this vital sector and redefine trans-Pacific travel for generations to come.
