Messages Revamped

A concept redesign of Apple Messages with enhanced features for better communication

Explore Demo

Know When to Reach Out

See your contacts' local time and location right in the conversation list. No more accidentally texting someone at 3 AM or wondering what timezone they're in.

Each contact shows their city and current local time, making it easy to communicate across the globe.

Send Messages at the Perfect Time

Schedule messages to be sent later with the beautiful iOS-style time picker. Perfect for sending birthday wishes at midnight, reminders at the right moment, or reaching out when your contact is awake.

Simply tap "Schedule Message" below the keyboard and spin the intuitive time wheels to select your preferred hour, minute, and AM/PM.

All Your Shared Photos in One Place

Never lose track of precious memories. The built-in photo gallery automatically collects every image shared in your conversations, displayed in a beautiful Apple Photos-style grid.

Tap the gallery icon in any chat to browse all shared photos, then tap any image to view it full-screen. It's the easiest way to find that photo someone sent you weeks ago.

Track Your Messaging Stats

Get insights into your conversations with detailed messaging statistics. See total messages exchanged, your longest streaks, and average response times for each contact.

Access stats from the filter menu to view a complete breakdown of your messaging activity, sorted by most active conversations. Perfect for seeing who you text the most.
